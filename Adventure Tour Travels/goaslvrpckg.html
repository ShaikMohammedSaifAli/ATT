    Calangute Beach

    Calangute Beach Known as the "Queen of Beaches," Calangute is one of the most popular and largest beaches in North Goa. It's famous for its golden sands, water sports activities, shacks serving delicious seafood, and vibrant nightlife.

    Activities To Be



      Parasailing offers an exhilarating experience as participants soar above the sea. With a parachute-like canopy, they enjoy breathtaking views of the beach and surroundings. Safety is paramount, with operators supplying harnesses and life jackets. Sessions typically last 10-15 minutes, providing ample time to savor the adventure. Prices vary based on factors like duration and season, ranging from ₹800 to ₹1500 per person.

      Banana Boat Rides offer group excitement as participants ride an inflatable banana-shaped boat towed by a speedboat. Riders enjoy bouncing and gliding over waves for a thrilling adventure. Safety is ensured with provided life jackets and thorough operator safety measures. Rides typically last 15-20 minutes, depending on the chosen package. Prices range from ₹300 to ₹500 per person, making it an affordable and fun water activity.
